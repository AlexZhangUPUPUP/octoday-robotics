## Repository Overview

`Octoday Robotics` is a curated embodied-AI resource repository covering fundamentals, companies, jobs, papers, code, datasets, benchmarks, and tools.

## Start Here

| Goal | Entry | What You Will Find |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Learn the field from scratch | [00-basics.md](00-basics.md) | books, courses, and onboarding fundamentals |
| Explore companies and the industry map | [01-companies.md](01-companies.md) | key domestic and global players |
| Track jobs and hiring signals | [02-jobs.md](02-jobs.md) | hiring demand, role types, and application notes |
| Follow papers, code, datasets, and benchmarks | [03-papers-code.md](03-papers-code.md) | research tracks, papers, datasets, benchmarks, surveys |
| Find tooling, simulators, frameworks, and SDKs | [04-tools.md](04-tools.md) | platforms, tooling, frameworks, and open-source stacks |
